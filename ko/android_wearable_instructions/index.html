For those devices the instructions are identical because they are directly connected to Google Fit

To connect Zepp Life with the app:

  • Connect your wristband or your smartwatch to the Zepp Life app
  • Start any workout. Before starting a workout, the app will prompt you to connect your wristband to track the data

If you are not prompted you should:

  1. Open the FitnessOnline app
  2. Tap on “More” in the bottom right corner
  3. Tap on “Settings”
  4. Tap on “Data exchange”
  5. Tap on the app’s icon
  6. Tap on “Connect”

If the wristband was previously connected but is not transferring data you should:

  1. Check that the wristband is connected to the Zepp Life app. To do this you will need to open the Zepp Life app, go to “Profile“ and ensure that the wristband is connected under “My devices”
  2. Go to the FitnessOnline app
  3. Tap on “More“ in the bottom right corner
  4. Tap on “Settings”
  5. Tap on “Data exchange”
  6. Tap on the app’s icon
  7. Tap on “Repeat connection”

If your wristband is connected but only a few calories have been counted:

This happens when the bracelet calculates the number of calories burned itself when the system workout is not turned on. To display a more accurate number of calories burned, start a workout on the wristband.

To connect Google Fit with the app:

  • Start any workout. Before starting a workout, you will be invited to pair if you already have the Google Fit app installed

If you are not prompted you should:

  1. Open the FitnessOnline app
  2. Tap on “More” in the bottom right corner
  3. Tap on “Settings”
  4. Tap on “Data exchange”
  5. Tap on the Google Fit icon
  6. Tap on “Sign in with Google”

If the data doesn’t appear in the report you should:

  1. Check if the data is being transferred to Google Fit

  2. Check the connection between Google Fit and the app, to do this complete the following steps in the FitnessOnline app:

    1. Tap on “More” in the bottom right corner
    2. Tap on “Settings”
    3. Tap on “Data exchange”
    4. Tap on the Google Fit icon

To connect Fitbit, LG, Polar with the app:

In order to connect your app used to track your activity with the FitnessOnline app you need to sync your app with Google Fit.

You should:

  1. Install the Google Fit app
  2. Create an account
  3. Open the app you use to track your activity
  4. Go to settings
  5. Find the section for connecting other apps. This is normally called “Connect other services”, “Apps and devices”, “Manage connections” etc.
  6. Follow the instructions to connect the app with Google Fit

Next, you need to sync Google Fit and the FitnessOnline app by doing the following:

  • Start any workout. Before starting a workout, you will be invited to pair

If you are not prompted you should:

  1. Open the FitnessOnline app
  2. Tap on “More” in the bottom right corner
  3. Tap on “Settings”
  4. Tap on “Data exchange”
  5. Tap on the Google Fit icon
  6. Tap on “Sign in with Google”

If the data doesn’t appear in the report you should:

  1. Check if the data is being transferred to Google Fit
  2. Check that Google Fit and the app are connected by going to Settings — Data exchange — Google Fit

In order to connect your app used to track your activity with Google Fit you should:

  1. Open the Google Fit app
  2. Tap on Profile — Tap on “Settings”
  3. Tap on “Manage connected apps”
  4. To open a list of all connected apps, press the down arrow under “All apps and devices”

To connect Huawei Health with FitnessOnline you will need to use Google Fit

To do this you will need to use the third-party app Health Sync (a paid-for app). If you are unable to use a paid-for app to sync data then you can return to the previous window and select sync with Google Fit (the wristband will not be taken into account).

To use a third-party app, you will need to do the following on Play Market:

  1. Install Google Fit
  2. Install the Health Sync app. Launch the app and select the Huawei Health Kit as the main source to sync and select Google Fit as the sync direction
  3. Tap on “Declaration of consent”

  4. Then you will need to follow the instructions on the Health Sync app, namely:

    • Tap on “Check Google account connection”
    • Tap on “Check activity tracking in Google Fit”
    • Install Huawei AppGallery (copy of instructions from the official website https://consumer.huawei.com/my/mobileservices/appgallery/android-installation)
    • Install HMS CORE (To do this open AppGallery and enter HMS CORE there). AppGallery will request permission to install from this product and permission must be granted
    • Install Huawei Health (or update)
    • Authenticate or log in to your Huawei account
    • Check the boxes necessary to sync the Health Sync app

  5. To connect Google Fit with the app:

    • Start any workout. Before starting a workout, you will be invited to pair if you already have the Google Fit app installed

If you are not prompted you should:

  1. Go to the FitnessOnline app
  2. Tap on “More” in the bottom right corner
  3. Tap on “Settings”
  4. Tap on “Data exchange”
  5. Tap on the Google Fit icon
  6. Tap on “Sign in with Google”

If the data doesn’t appear in the report you should:

  1. Check if the data is being transferred to Google Fit
  2. Check that Google Fit and the app are connected by going to Settings — Data exchange — Google Fit
