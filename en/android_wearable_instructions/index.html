These devices have a direct connection to the Health Connect app

To sync Google Health Connect (available in FitnessOnline versions 2.20.0 and higher) with the app, follow these steps:

  1. Install the Health Connect app on your device.
  2. After the initial installation of FitnessOnline on your device and after signing in to your account, grant access to read data in Health Connect.

If access to read data in Health Connect was not granted after the initial installation of the app:

  1. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
  2. Tap “Settings”.
  3. Tap “Data sharing”.
  4. Tap the Health Connect icon.
  5. Tap “Open settings”.
  6. Grant access to read data for the FitnessOnline app.

If data is not displayed on the workout summary screen, follow these steps:

  1. Check if the data is being synced to Health Connect.

  2. Verify the sync status between Health Connect and FitnessOnline by doing the following in the FitnessOnline app:

    1. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
    2. Tap “Settings”.
    3. Tap “Data sharing”.
    4. Tap the Health Сonnect icon.

To sync Zepp Life with FitnessOnline:

  1. Connect your fitness band or smartwatch to the Zepp Life app.
  2. Start any workout in FitnessOnline. Before the workout, the app will prompt you to sync your fitness band for data tracking.

If the sync prompt does not appear, follow these steps:

  1. Open the FitnessOnline app.
  2. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
  3. Tap “Settings”.
  4. Tap “Data sharing”.
  5. Tap the app’s icon.
  6. Tap “Connect”.

If your fitness band is connected but data is not syncing:

  1. Turn Bluetooth off and back on.
  2. Check the sync status of your fitness band with the Zepp Life app. To do this, open the Zepp Life app, go to the “Profile” tab, and in the “My Devices” section, make sure your fitness band is connected.
  3. Open the FitnessOnline app.
  4. Tap “More“ in the bottom right corner.
  5. Tap “Settings”.
  6. Tap “Data sharing”.
  7. Tap the app’s icon.
  8. Tap “Reconnect”.

If your fitness band is connected but shows low calories on the workout summary screen:

This happens because your fitness band calculates that specific amount of burned calories.

For a more accurate display of calories burned, you should start a system workout on your fitness band.

To sync Google Fit (available in FitnessOnline versions 2.19.2 and below) with the app, follow these steps:

  1. Install the Google Fit app on your device.
  2. Start any workout in FitnessOnline. Before the workout, the app will prompt you to sync with Google Fit for data tracking.

If the sync prompt does not appear, follow these steps:

  1. Open the FitnessOnline app.
  2. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
  3. Tap “Settings”.
  4. Tap “Data sharing”.
  5. Tap the icon for Google Fit.
  6. Tap “Sign in with Google”.

If data is not displayed on the workout summary screen, follow these steps:

  1. Check if the data is being synced to Google Fit.

  2. Verify the sync status between Google Fit and FitnessOnline by doing the following in the FitnessOnline app:

    1. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
    2. Tap “Settings”.
    3. Tap “Data sharing”.
    4. Tap the Google Fit icon.

Data transfer from Samsung Health, Garmin, Fitbit, and Polar Flow to FitnessOnline is facilitated through Google Health Connect

To connect, follow these steps:

  1. Install the Health Connect app and sign in.
  2. Open the app used for tracking your activity.
  3. Go to the settings menu.
  4. Locate the section for connecting third-party apps. This is usually labeled as “Connect Other Services”, “Apps and Devices”, “Manage Connections” or something similar.
  5. Follow the instructions to connect the app to Health Connect.

Next, sync Health Connect with the FitnessOnline app as follows:

  1. In the FitnessOnline app, tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
  2. Tap “Settings”.
  3. Tap “Data sharing”.
  4. Tap the Health Connect icon.
  5. Tap “Open settings”.
  6. Grant access to read data for the FitnessOnline app.

If data is not displayed on the workout summary screen, follow these steps:

  1. Verify if data from the app used for activity tracking is being synced to Health Connect.

  2. Verify the sync status between Health Connect and FitnessOnline by doing the following in the FitnessOnline app:

    1. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
    2. Tap “Settings”.
    3. Tap “Data sharing”.
    4. Tap the Health Сonnect icon.

To check the connection between Health Connect and the app used for activity tracking, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Health Connect app.
  2. In the “Permissions and data” section, tap “App permissions”.
  3. Ensure that the app you are using is listed under “Allowed access”.

If your fitness band and Health Connect are connected, but incorrect activity data or no data appears on the workout summary screen:

This happens because the activity tracking app sends specific calorie, step count, and heart rate data to Health Connect.

FitnessOnline only displays data received from Health Connect.

To ensure more accurate activity data is displayed on the workout summary screen:

  1. Before starting a workout in FitnessOnline, begin a system workout on your fitness band.
  2. Before ending the workout in FitnessOnline, open the app used for activity tracking and refresh the sync between your fitness band and mobile device.

Data transfer from Huawei Health app to FitnessOnline is facilitated through Google Health Connect and Health Sync (requires a paid subscription)

To connect, follow these steps:

  1. Install the Health Connect app and sign in.
  2. Install the Health Sync app. Open the app and select Huawei Health Kit as the primary sync source; for the sync direction, choose Health Connect.
  3. Tap the “Consent Statement” button.

  4. Then, follow the instructions in the Health Sync app:

    • Tap “Check Connection to Google Account”.
    • Tap “Check Activity Tracking in Health Connect”.
    • Install Huawei AppGallery (refer to the official instructions: Huawei Support).
    • Install HMS CORE from AppGallery (AppGallery will require system permission to install third-party apps).
    • Install or update Huawei Health.
    • Sign in to your Huawei account.
    • In Health Sync, check the boxes for the types of data you want to transfer to Health Connect.
  5. Ensure that Health Sync is successfully connected to Huawei Health in the “Privacy management” settings of the Huawei Health app.

Next, sync Health Connect with the FitnessOnline app as follows:

  1. In the FitnessOnline app, tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
  2. Tap “Settings”.
  3. Tap “Data sharing”.
  4. Tap the Health Connect icon.
  5. Tap “Open settings”.
  6. Grant access to read data for the FitnessOnline app.

If data is not displayed on the workout summary screen, follow these steps:

  1. Verify if data from Health Sync is being synced to Health Connect.

  2. Verify the sync status between Health Connect and FitnessOnline by doing the following in the FitnessOnline app:

    1. Tap “More” in the bottom right corner.
    2. Tap “Settings”.
    3. Tap “Data sharing”.
    4. Tap the Health Сonnect icon.

To check the connection between Health Connect and Health Sync, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Health Connect app.
  2. In the “Permissions and data” section, tap “App permissions”.
  3. Ensure that Health Sync is listed under “Allowed access”.

If your fitness band, Health Sync, and Health Connect are connected, but incorrect activity data or no data appears on the workout summary screen:

This occurs because the activity tracking app sends specific calorie, step count, and heart rate data to Health Sync. Also, Health Sync may transmit data to Health Connect with a delay.

FitnessOnline displays only the data received from Health Connect.

To ensure more accurate activity data is displayed on the workout summary screen, follow these steps:

  1. Before starting a workout in FitnessOnline, begin a system workout on your fitness band.
  2. Before finishing the workout in FitnessOnline, open the app used for activity tracking and refresh the sync between your fitness band and mobile device.
  3. Open the Health Sync app and refresh the sync with Health Connect.
  4. Return to the FitnessOnline app and complete the workout.
